### Multiple Choice Questions (Medium Difficulty)  

1. Which of the following statements correctly defines a chemical reaction?  
   (a) A process where substances are mixed without any change in composition.  
   (b) A process where one or more substances are transformed into new substances with different properties.  
   (c) A change that only alters the state of matter.  
   (d) A process that does not involve breaking or forming chemical bonds.  

2. In a balanced chemical equation, the principle that is obeyed is:  
   (a) Law of Definite Proportions  
   (b) Law of Conservation of Mass  
   (c) Law of Multiple Proportions  
   (d) Law of Radioactive Decay  

3. Which type of reaction involves two or more reactants combining to form a single product?  
   (a) Decomposition reaction  
   (b) Displacement reaction  
   (c) Combination reaction  
   (d) Double displacement reaction  

4. Which of the following is an example of an exothermic reaction?  
   (a) Photosynthesis  
   (b) Melting of ice  
   (c) Combustion of methane  
   (d) Evaporation of water  

5. When an acid reacts with a base in aqueous solution, the typical products formed are a salt and ______.  
   (a) Oxygen gas  
   (b) Water  
   (c) Hydrogen gas  
   (d) Carbon dioxide
